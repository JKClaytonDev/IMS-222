How To Make French Toast

French toast is a tasty way to start the day!
Learn how to make French Toast to make your mornings just a little sweeter.

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 4 slices of bread
  • Milk

Instructions:

  1. Mix egg, vanilla & cinnamon in dish. Stir in milk.
  2. Dip bread in egg mixture, turning to coat both sides evenly.
  3. Cook bread slices on lightly greased nonstick griddle or skillet on medium heat until browned on both sides. Serve with Easy Spiced Syrup (recipe follows), if desired./li>
  4. Add teaspoon of Vanilla Extract and 1/4 teaspoon of Ground Cinnamon to 1 cup pancake syrup. Stir well to mix.
